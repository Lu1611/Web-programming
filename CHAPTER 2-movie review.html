Overview

Poor Things (2023) is a dark comedy directed by Yorgos Lanthimos, based on the novel by Alasdair Gray. The film follows the bizarre life of Bella Baxter, a young woman brought back to life by a brilliant but unorthodox scientist. As Bella navigates a strange new world, she embarks on a journey of self-discovery, love, and rebellion. The film is a blend of gothic horror, absurd humor, and social commentary.

Key Information

  • Director: Yorgos Lanthimos
  • Release Date: 2023
  • Genre: Dark Comedy, Drama
  • Runtime: 141 minutes
  • Studio: Searchlight Pictures

Characters

The film features an eccentric mix of characters, with Bella Baxter being the central figure. Bella, portrayed by Emma Stone, is a young woman who was resurrected by a scientist and grapples with her new existence. Other notable characters include Dr. Gottfried von Klemmer, the eccentric scientist played by Willem Dafoe, and Max McCandles, portrayed by Ramy Youssef, who becomes entwined in Bella’s strange journey.

Reviews
